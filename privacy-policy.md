# Privacy Policy — Outlook Calendar Sync

## What data we access
- Google Calendar: read and create events

## How we use data
- Only to sync events from Microsoft Outlook to Google Calendar
- No data is sent to third parties
- No data is stored on servers — everything works locally on your computer

## Data retention
- OAuth token is stored locally on the user's device
- Token can be deleted by clicking "Disconnect" in the plugin
